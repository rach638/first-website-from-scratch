A Winter Poem About Rock Creek Park

By Rachel Kellner

I walked the Western Ridge trail hoping

the height would bring me toward God
and some great hawk would fly beside me
and show me how to be strong.

A big soaring thing did pass me by, too

quickly to identify, but she hid
among the branches far above the trail,
far above where I could climb,
her answers blocked from my sight.
